Furniture design and make My furniture is designed and made with their own unique individual characteristics. These are seen in the final product through the design, which expresses the clients personality from their design appearance to their personal functional use. Each piece of furniture is made to enhance the beauty of the wood, through the use of subtle but elegant soft lines and the use of handmade joints to create greater strength and a visual attraction to show its quality and appearance. Attention to detail gives each piece it's own unique featured design, which is constructed to best suit the clients product.
Furniture restoration of modern and antique furniture Each piece of furniture that is restored is finished to the specification of the clients needs, whether it is to be fixed from having broken parts that need to be replaced, has loose joints that need to be fixed or glue back together, or if it needs to be stripped and re polished with normal or french polishing.
